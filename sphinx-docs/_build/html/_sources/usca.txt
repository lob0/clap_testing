==================
What is an USCA ?
==================

.. _USCA: http://weareclap.herokuapp.com/uminho


`USCA`_ is where you can find the services we provide, which are at the moment, the food information and the map service.
This includes all the information concerning the restaurants that are already partners of us, including the menus. 
Furthermore, it is in `USCA`_ that you can sing up to enjoy our services. 

Stay connected for we are constantly updating our services!
